Because Choose us?

We are an inbound tourism agency offering transfer services, regular tours, and private tours throughout the Rio de Janeiro region. We serve both end consumers and international agencies seeking a local partner to cater to their passengers.

Our story

With the goal of helping others discover Rio de Janeiro, we founded Rio Imperio Turismo in 2017. Today, with a team of over 15 people taking care of every detail, we have established ourselves as a travel agency that stands out for its excellent customer service; we inspire confidence, provide respect, and offer support to the hundreds of travelers who contact us every day. Furthermore, with our aim to be agents of change, we have taken on the task of setting trends in the city. This demands great responsibility, discipline, and commitment.

Trust and responsibility

We have dedicated time and effort to finding and selecting the companies that work with us. And we chose them according to the highest criteria... our own. Because we work every day as if it were the first, knowing that we would love to meet people like us anywhere in the world, spreading joy, conveying respect for tourism and the environment, and above all, upholding such noble values as trust, responsibility, and truth.

Wide variety

Over the years, we have developed a portfolio of services designed to include tours and experiences that blend culture, stunning locations, and breathtaking landscapes. We also offer personalized activities tailored to each client's preferences and budget, always adhering to the high standards and quality they deserve.
